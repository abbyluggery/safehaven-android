## Partnership Tiers

### Tier 1: Endorsement Partners
**Benefits**: Logo on website, "Partner-approved" badge in app
**Requirements**: Sign MOU, promote SafeHaven to constituents
**Examples**: NNEDV, NCAVP, NIWRC

### Tier 2: Referral Partners
**Benefits**: Listed in resource database, priority support
**Requirements**: Meet vetting criteria, provide service to survivors
**Examples**: Local DV shelters, legal aid clinics

### Tier 3: Technology Partners
**Benefits**: Co-development opportunities, data insights (anonymized)
**Requirements**: Provide tech infrastructure, maintain privacy standards
**Examples**: Google.org, AWS

## MOU Templates

### Standard Partnership MOU

```
MEMORANDUM OF UNDERSTANDING

Between: SafeHaven ("App Provider")
And: [Partner Organization] ("Partner")

WHEREAS, SafeHaven provides a free safety planning app for domestic
violence survivors, with a focus on marginalized communities;

WHEREAS, Partner provides [services] to survivors of domestic violence;

NOW THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

1. PARTNERSHIP OBJECTIVES
   SafeHaven and Partner will collaborate to:
   a) Improve access to technology for survivors
   b) Ensure culturally competent resources
   c) Advance survivor safety and autonomy

2. SAFEHAVEN RESPONSIBILITIES
   a) Provide free app access to Partner's clients
   b) Offer staff training (2-hour workshop)
   c) Include Partner in resource database (if applicable)
   d) Quarterly impact reports

3. PARTNER RESPONSIBILITIES
   a) Promote SafeHaven to appropriate clients
   b) Provide feedback on app usability
   c) Participate in annual survey
   d) Maintain confidentiality of survivor data

4. DATA & PRIVACY
   a) Partner will NOT share survivor data with SafeHaven
   b) SafeHaven uses zero-knowledge encryption (cannot access data)
   c) All data sharing requires explicit survivor consent

5. TERM & TERMINATION
   a) Term: 1 year from signature date
   b) Renewable annually
   c) Either party may terminate with 30 days notice

6. NO FINANCIAL OBLIGATION
   This partnership involves no exchange of funds.

SIGNED:

_______________________          Date: __________
SafeHaven Executive Director

_______________________          Date: __________
[Partner] Authorized Representative
```
