markdown# SafeHaven Legal Compliance

## 501(c)(3) Requirements
- IRS Form 1023-EZ ($275 fee)
- Board of directors (5 members, majority independent)
- Bylaws, conflict of interest policy
- Annual Form 990

## Privacy & Security
- GDPR/CCPA compliance
- Zero-knowledge encryption
- No data sharing (even with court subpoena)
- Privacy policy, terms of service

## Liability Protection
- D&O insurance ($2K/year)
- General liability ($1K/year)
- Cyber insurance ($1K/year)
- Hold harmless clauses
